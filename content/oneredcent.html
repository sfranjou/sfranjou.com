About Us

Anchored around Mariabelle's soulful voice, we play classic soul music and its heritage, from Funk and RnB to Disco and Neo-Soul.
The band met in MIT's jazz program and had been playing together for years before hearing Mariabelle.
We were hooked at the first note and we think you'll be too!
Since forming, we've performed with Lupe Fiasco for a thousand people in Kresge Auditorium, lit up the Fairmont Copley Plaza hotel ballroom, and played driveways, stairs, and Porch(fest)s of every size and shape.
