Welcome to my blog! I finally got around to adding one to this site. It's a simple setup — just HTML files and a comment section powered by a tiny serverless function.

Why a blog?

I wanted a place to write about music, software, and the weird intersection of the two that I find myself in professionally. Social media is fine for short-form stuff, but sometimes you need more than 280 characters.
